Auto Invest Dividends

When enabled, dividends are automatically invested instead of going to your current account.

This means:

  • Dividends will be added directly to your investment account
  • Your investment will grow faster through compound interest
  • You don't need to manually deposit dividends

Tip: This is especially useful for maximizing long-term growth!

Auto Invest Rent

When enabled, property income is automatically invested instead of going to your current account.

This means:

  • Property rent will be added directly to your investment account
  • Your investment will grow faster through compound interest
  • You don't need to manually deposit property income

Tip: This is especially useful for maximizing long-term growth from your properties!
